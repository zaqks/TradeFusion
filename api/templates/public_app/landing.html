Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for importing products?

To import products, you need an import license, a certificate of conformity, and various other documents such as a commercial invoice, packing list, and customs declaration.

How does the automated tax system work?

Our automated tax system calculates the applicable taxes based on your transaction details and deducts them automatically during the payment process.

Can I track my shipments in real-time?

Yes! Our platform provides real-time tracking for all shipments, allowing you to monitor their status at any time.
